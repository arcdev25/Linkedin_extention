# Profile Checker Panel Guide

## Overview
The Profile Checker panel appears automatically when you visit LinkedIn profile pages (`linkedin.com/in/...`). The panel is now **movable and minimizable** for your convenience.

## Panel Controls

### Moving the Panel
1. Click and hold the **header bar** (the dark blue area with "◈ Profile Checker")
2. Drag the panel to any position on the page
3. Release to drop it in place
4. The panel will stay in that position as you scroll

### Minimizing/Maximizing
- Click the **"−" button** in the top-right to minimize (collapse) the panel
- When minimized, only the header bar is visible
- Click the **"+" button** to maximize (expand) the panel again
- The panel remembers its position when minimized

### Closing the Panel
- Click the **"×" button** in the top-right corner
- The panel will close with a smooth fade-out animation
- It will reappear automatically when you visit another LinkedIn profile

## Features Available in the Panel

### Your Outreach Section
- **Status Dropdown**: Set the contact status (Pending, Chatting, Interested, etc.)
- **Notes Field**: Add private notes about this contact
- **Add/Update Button**: Save your changes to the database

### Highlights Section
- **Text Selection**: Select any text on the LinkedIn page to highlight it
- **Color Picker**: Choose from 5 highlight colors
- **Notes**: Optionally add notes to your highlights
- **View Highlights**: See all your saved highlights for this profile

### Other Recruiters Section
- See which other team members have contacted this profile
- View their status, notes, and contact dates
- Helps avoid duplicate outreach

## Tips

- **Position the panel** where it doesn't block important profile information
- **Minimize it** when you need more screen space but want quick access
- **Drag it to the side** if you're reading the profile details
- The panel **auto-loads** when you navigate to a new LinkedIn profile

## Troubleshooting

**Panel appears in the wrong position?**
- Just drag it to where you want it
- The position is reset for each new profile

**Can't drag the panel?**
- Make sure you're clicking on the header bar, not the buttons
- Avoid clicking directly on the "−" or "×" buttons

**Panel disappeared?**
- It may have been closed - navigate to another LinkedIn profile to see it again
- Or refresh the current profile page
